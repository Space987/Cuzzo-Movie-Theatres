Cuzzo Movie Theater ™



About ☺

Cinémas Cuzzo is a regional chain of movie theaters located in the Canadian province of Quebec. There are currently 10 Méga-Plex locations and 1 Cinema location, all within the greater Montreal area.

History ℍ

Cinemas Cuzzo was started by Axel Cuzzo shortly after immigrating to Canada. He was born in France in 1975. In 2000, Natan Cuzzo was born and remains the Cuzzo's only child. Natan has had 15 children (Now at 20!). After serving as the company's executive vice-president since 2000, as of 2018, Natan took on the position of president. Natan is also often quoted saying, "I am extremely gratfull that i am able to do the thing i love everday while making other peoples day".

Food 🍫

As well as offering a comfertable movie expirience. Cuzzo also gives its customers a variety of tasty snack that they are able to eat while they watch their movie. These snacks include delicious Popcorn and a multitude of chocolates such as Mars, Bounty, Mike and Ikes, and Hersey. What makes it even better is that all chocolate are at a resonable price of $5.99

Location ✈

Since it's creation, Cuzzo has had the ability to open up 5 different Cinemas all over the greater Montreal area. The first location that opened was in 2000 in Terrebonne, QC and its most recent Cinema that opened in 2012 in Saint-Laurent region.
